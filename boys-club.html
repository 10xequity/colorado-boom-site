Boys Club Volleyball

Ages 13U–18U  |  Season: September – February & June  |  Sanctioned by RMR and USAV

Boys Program Overview

Competitive levels available: National, Regional, Local, and Developmental. Players compete from September through February, with an optional June extension for national-championship teams.

More Touches, More Growth

Practice: 5:00–7:30 PM Three days a week (M/W/F or T/Th/F) · one location, all season
  • Home for dinner. Every practice wraps by 7:30 PM — no late-night sessions.
  • Families and players first. Predictable times and one location you can plan your life around.
  • 2.5-hour sessions, 3 days/week (M/W/F or T/Th/F).
  • Integrated 30-minute conditioning block at the end of practice.
  • Friday: dedicated scrimmage and position-specific sessions.
  • One location always — no bouncing between facilities.
  • Makeup practices on alternate days.
  • Multi-sport athletes can reschedule to attend other days' practices.
  • Boomtown drop-in included for all club members.
